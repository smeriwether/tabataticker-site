Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

Overview

Tabata Ticker is built to be simple and private. The app runs a fixed interval timer on your device and does not require an account.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information through the Tabata Ticker app or this website.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use analytics in the app or on this website.
  • We do not use cookies on this website.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, workouts, or timer history.
  • We do not sell or share personal data.

Local Data

The app may store local preferences, such as whether sound cues are enabled. Those preferences remain on your device.

Apple Watch

When paired with Apple Watch, the iPhone owns the timer and the Watch mirrors timer state while sending control commands. This communication happens between your own devices.

App Store

When Tabata Ticker is available through the App Store, downloads and any App Store account information will be handled by Apple under Apple's privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@merimerimeri.com.
